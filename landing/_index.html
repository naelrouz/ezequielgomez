Ezequiel Gomez

Ezequiel Gomez Dancer, teacher and choreographer dancing Tango Argentino - It began in 1997 as a dancer Folkdance Argentinas, achieving first prize in competitions at the provincial level.
- It was developed as a teacher at the Institute of Folklore Luis Federico Leloir and Educational Institute in San Miguel (parish) in the period 2001 ~ 05.
- 2005 organized its first folk festival in the city of San Miguel (her native city), the event was named cultural interest by the Ministry of Culture of the City of San Miguel, province of Buenos Aires.
- In 2006 the month two of the first day was invited to join the staff of DNI Tango as a teacher and dancer. With the company DNI TANGO directed by Paul Villarraza and Dana Frígoli, he has participated in the 1st and 2nd International Tango Festival de la Plata, 3rd Festival of Dance and Cambalache theater show "The Collection, Los Tangos of a Life "at the theater on February 3rd city of Parana the November 1, 2007. 4th and 6th Tango dance and in the most important milongas of Buenos Aires World; The ideal, Salon Canning, Chip, The tile and Sunderland with the company DNI TANGO. 2nd Octopus Tango Week Festival. With Horacio Ferrer Garcia organized a fusion of tango, theater and poetry in the studio DNI TANGO.
- In 2009 the city of tokyo-japan is rooted to work in the studio Zero Hour directed by John Guida. Workshop conducted in major cities in Japan, Nagoya, Osaka, Kyoto, Kanagawa, Shizuoka. Participate in the bicentennial of Argentina organized by the Argentina Embassy in Japan. RED TANGO with Ezequiel Farfaro artist and theater director Francisco Leiva.
In the same añofue invited as teacher honor of St. Peters burg (Russia) to teach classes and do exhibitions.
- In 2010 TANGO BAR with Alejandra Gutty, Workshop folklore. It was the first Argentine tango workshop gave in Khabarovsk (Russia).
- During 2010 and 2013 he was invited to teach at the different countries of Asia, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and Indonesia.
- In 2013 he won the 2nd place in the competition held in Japan every year Asiato Argentine Tango Championship.
- In his present one he is working in Japan as a teacher, choreographer and dancer.

His dance character, strength and presence unfolds. In her dancing you can appreciate a traditional closed along with modern and contemporary.
